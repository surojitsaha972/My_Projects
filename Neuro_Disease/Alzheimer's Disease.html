Neurodegenerative Diseases

Alzheimer's Disease (AD)

It is a progressive brain disorder that causes memory loss, cognitive decline, and behavioral changes. It is the most common cause of dementia, leading to difficulties in daily life. It results from abnormal protein buildup in the brain, damaging cells over time.
